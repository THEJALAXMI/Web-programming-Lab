ICET

Established in 2002, ICET nurtures engineering graduates with academic excellence and holistic development.With 23 years of legacy, ICET has been committed to fostering academic excellence, facilitating career advancement, promoting entrepreneurial initiatives, and providing scholarship opportunities to students.ICET has been one of the pioneering self financing colleges in Kerala. It is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University(KTU).Many of our alumni have paved themselves worthwhile as successful entrepreneurs. With a strong commitment to social responsibility, ICET offers scholarships and free education through various agencies, empowering future leaders in engineering and technology.
